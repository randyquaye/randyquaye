"Ubuntu" translated from Zulu means 'I am because we are".

Deeply invested in enriching community, the Ubuntu network is a social enterprise that aims to provide information to international students enabling them to navigate the UK labour market. We are devoted to developing a sense of Community among international students, providing those interested, with the Information and Opportunity to gain work experience here in the UK.

Community

A comunity that values and empowers international students

Information

Excellent content on our mobile application, events and blogs to server international students.

Opportunity

Platform that offers visibility and clarity to the plight of international students seeking jobs in the UK.

Check out the App features

Our app seeks to create a common place where information is shared and discussed by members. But also to share in the success of each others and foster a true sense of community.

Social Feed

Interact, share, and learn from fellow Ubuntu Network members on our social feed. The more you give, the more you get! This is alsp where you will be able to keep up with all the latest posts and events hosted by the Ubuntu Network and our partners

Blog Feed

From interviews with young professionals to the latest news on government legislation regarding international students, our blogs will keep you informed and help you on your journey as an international students and young professional in the UK job market.

Jobs Catalogue

Browse jobs from varying industries and companies with our job catalogue. Search for jobs in companies and industries that are able and willing to sponsor you for a Tier 2 Visa (right to work in the UK). You can also see what people currently employed in certain jobs are saying about it, and you can ask them any questions you have regarding this employment.
