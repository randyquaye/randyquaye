TEED Ghana offers consultancy services to government agencies and schools on teaching, learning and curriculum development. RECRUITING

Our focus is to recruit qualified individuals to teach in rural areas and public schools in an effort to bridge the educational and ultimately socio-economic gap in Ghana. Volunteer positions are available to eligible high school and university students.

TEED FELLOWSHIP PROGRAMME

University students and recent graduates can apply to teach in rural areas and public schools for one year as TEED Fellows. Successful applicants will be paid a monthly stipend, receive in-depth training, get connected with a mentor, and experience the opportunity to explore a different part of Ghana while impacting lives. Recent graduates will also be are eligible to complete their National Service through the TEED Program.

ORPHANAGE PLEDGE

Orphans in Ghana are particularly disadvantaged as there is no formal social structure to absorb and cater for them. As such, they have to rely on benevolent individuals, orphanage homes and family members to pursue their education. In alignment with our overall vision of development through education, this programme helps free enrollment of qualifying candidates into various educational institutions around the country. Learn how to participate in the Orphan pledge as an educational institution, orphanage or individual.

Literacy Initiatives
