Frequently Asked Questions

RECRUITING

What is the timeline of the 1 year fellowship?

The fellowship begins with training on the third Monday in July for 6 weeks, placement in August, teaching from September through July and a debriefing and handover session to new fellows in the 3rd week of July. Monthly check in with supervisor are compulsory as well as all fellowship events. Failure to comply with regulations and requirements will result in a refund of all stipends.

Is there a stipend for holiday volunteers?

Due to limited resources, only fellows who make a commitment to the 1 year program receive stipends.

Can I earn academic or extracurricular credit?

TEED Ghana is able to provide a letter of reference for work done with us, however it is up to each academic institution to determine whether this qualifies for academic/extra curricular credit.
